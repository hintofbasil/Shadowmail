Frequently Asked Questions
play_arrow What is ShadowMail?
ShadowMail is a private email forwarding service. Any email sent to a ShadowMail address will be forwarded on to your personal email address.
play_arrow Why did you create this service?
Have you ever given your email address to a website only to start recieving spam? ShadowMail is my solution, by giving each website a different email address you can see which website has sold or lost your details. You may then delete that single email address avoiding all spam while keeping your email account intact.
play_arrow Does this service cost money?
No. This is a service created for personal use instead of financial gain. I have simply decided to open it up to the world.
play_arrow Do you read or record emails?
No. I strongly believe in privacy. Your emails are not saved, read, or otherwise processed. The only thing I do is append a delete link to the end. All of our code is open source and I encourage users to check our methodologies.
play_arrow Are there any limits in place?
Yes, currently you may only create 3 emails addresses per hour. This is limited by both receiveing address and IP address. I will explore removing or increasing these limits in the future.
play_arrow Do you accept donations?
Currently no. This is something I may consider in the future depending on the popularity of the service. Either way I intend for this service to remain free for everyone to use.
